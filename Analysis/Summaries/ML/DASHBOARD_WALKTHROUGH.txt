          CRIMESCRAPE COMMAND CENTER: DASHBOARD USER GUIDE

Product: Premium NCRB Crime Intelligence Dashboard
Platform: CrimeScrape v3.0
Document Date: April 15, 2026

I. SIDEBAR NAVIGATION
---------------------
The dashboard is controlled via a high-aesthetic sidebar on the left:
- Dashboard (Overview): The centralized hub for national-level metrics.
- ML Research: Deep-dive into internal model visualizations.
- Regional Map: Interactive geospatial intelligence layer.
- Comparison Lab: Side-by-side behavioral analysis of two states.
- Risk Predictor: Individual state forecasting and threat assessment.

II. TAB 1: EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW
-----------------------------
The landing page for critical metrics:
- Stats Grid: Shows the total processed records (71,600+) and flags the current statistical "Red-Zone" (Delhi UT).
- National Intensity Heatmap: A high-level visualization showing how crime density varies across categories (Women, Children, etc.) at a glance.
- Latest Intelligence Report: A live window scrolling through the core findings of our EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis).

III. TAB 2: ML RESEARCH GALLERY
-------------------------------
A gallery of the model-specific outputs generated during the training phase:
- Archetypes: Visualization of DBSCAN clusters (identifying which states share a similar crime DNA).
- Drivers: Bar charts of "Feature Importance" (showing which specific law sections, like 498A or 354, are the biggest contributors to the overall crime rate).

IV. TAB 3: REGIONAL MAP (GEOSPATIAL LAYER)
------------------------------------------
An interactive choropleth map powered by Leaflet.js:
- Domain Selector: Toggle between Women, Children, Juvenile, and Trafficking maps.
- Interactive Polygons: Every state in India is colored by its "Predicted 2026 Intensity."
- Popups: Click or Hover on a state to see its name and its specific predicted crime index.
- Legend: Colors range from Light Yellow (Lowest) to Deep Red (Highest intensity).

V. TAB 4: COMPARISON LAB (HEAD-TO-HEAD)
---------------------------------------
A comparative simulation tool:
- State Selectors: Two dropdown menus allow you to choose any two regions (e.g., Bihar vs. Kerala).
- Joint Analysis: Clicking "Initialize" runs the prediction models for both states simultaneously.
- Results: Displays side-by-side Intensity Indices and Risk Grades for a direct "Safety Benchmarking."

VI. TAB 5: THREAT FORECASTING CENTER (PREDICTOR)
-----------------------------------------------
The most advanced part of the dashboard for individual target analysis:
- Controls: Select a State and a Category.
- Output Card: Generates a high-end Glassmorphism result card.
- Metrics:
    * Predicted Intensity Index (2026): A forecast of crimes per 100k population.
    * Risk Grade (Sentinel): A "HIGH RISK" or "STABLE" badge based on national benchmarks.
    * Subtitles: Each result now includes a short definition to help you interpret the numbers.

VII. GLOSSARY OF TERMS
-----------------------
- INTENSITY INDEX: Crimes per 100,000 population. Used to compare big states fairly with small ones.
- RISK GRADE: A binary classification label. If a state's "Intensity" is higher than the national median, it is flagged as "HIGH RISK."
